Our Goals

Our goals with creating Slurp Slop is to bring back the era of having to do authentic research and to save our planet.
We feel that too many people have become reliant on AI and other low quality resources that they see on the internet.
Because of that, the things we trust online for our day to day lives are dwindling. We also realize that tons of people are concerned
about the environmental effects that AI has on our planet. Because of this, we developed an algorithm that not only cuts ou the actual
web page element of the Gemini AI overview, but also stops the web page from reaching out to the server, effectively stopping the negative power use.

We truly do hope that people will use our extension to not only help bring back a sense of academic wellness, but fight against the environmental defects of AI.

Features

Whenever you choose to open up the popup overview, you will be presented with different toggleable features for the entire extension. These options consist of:
  • -Enable or Disable the entire extension
  • -Turning on/off the AI overview, ads, low quality websites, and the AI mode button
  • -Minimalist mode (Just removes the AI overview)
  • -Aggresive Mode (Blocks network requests for AI content)
  • -Links Only (Removes every component except links)
  • -Placeholders (Replaces removed content with placeholders)
  • -Logging
